About the Designer

"Design isn't decoration — it's the language a brand uses to build trust before anyone says a word."

Based in Barrie, Ontario, I'm a graphic designer and brand strategist working with startups, studios, and entrepreneurs across North America. Since 2023 I've been building visual identities, digital experiences, and brand systems that help businesses grow with confidence.

I believe every detail matters — from the weight of a typeface to the timing of a hover state. My work sits at the intersection of craft and strategy, where aesthetics serve a purpose.
