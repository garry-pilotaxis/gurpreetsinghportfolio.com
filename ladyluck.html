Project Overview

"Beauty that feels like fortune — refined, feminine, forever."

LADYluck is a premium skincare and beauty brand built around the idea of effortless elegance. The brief called for a visual identity that felt both timeless and contemporary — luxurious without being inaccessible, bold without losing its femininity.

The brand system spans wordmark design, product packaging, advertising, storefront signage, and digital assets, all anchored in a signature purple palette and a refined editorial tone.
